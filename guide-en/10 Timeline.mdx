haoone's subtitle track is overlaid on the audio waveform, making it convenient to quickly adjust subtitle block time alignment with audio.

### Play/Pause

Press Spacebar

### Adjust Subtitle Start and End Time

On the timeline, you can drag to adjust the subtitle time range:

![#w300](../image/iShot_2026-01-09_12.36.28.png)

### B Shortcut Key

Using the B shortcut key, you can split the currently highlighted subtitle block at the playhead position, making splitting very convenient

### Global Subtitle Preview Area

Above the timeline is a colorful line, which is the global subtitle preview area. Different subtitle blocks use different colors for distinction. You can click on a block, and the timeline will scroll to that subtitle block's position.

![#w800](../image/iShot_2026-01-09_15.03.08.png)
