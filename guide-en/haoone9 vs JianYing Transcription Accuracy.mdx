Compared to older versions, haoone 9 has upgraded both remote transcription and local transcription models. Chinese and English recognition accuracy has surpassed JianYing, achieving word-level high-precision alignment.

(Thanks to Alibaba's open-source qwen3-asr model)

haoone local transcription Chinese recognition accuracy is higher than JianYing. English recognition accuracy is similar to JianYing. Dialect recognition is significantly better than JianYing.

haoone remote transcription Chinese recognition accuracy is higher than both JianYing and haoone local transcription. English recognition accuracy is better than both JianYing and haoone local transcription. Dialect recognition is significantly better than both JianYing and haoone local transcription.

haoone remote transcription's running speed, accuracy, hallucination control, noise resistance, and word-level alignment rate are better than all subtitle software on the market in 2026.

haoone's model still needs further enhancement in dialect recognition and English song recognition.
